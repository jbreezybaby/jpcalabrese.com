🎓 NOVA BLUES JAM 101

WHAT TO EXPECT

Open to musicians of all skill levels. We'll teach you the basics in person. The jam takes place at the Harris Pavilion in historic downtown Manassas — a large public space with tables, chairs, shade, and rain cover.

Currently running as an electric jam. Strangers have been known to wander over, grab a harmonica, and sit in for a song. Dancers too.

"If you've never had the blues, just keep living."

NEW TO THE BLUES?

Start here. Once you're comfortable, dig into dominant 7 chords, the pentatonic scale, the blues scale, and classic blues songs.

  • Intro to the 12 bar blues progression
  • 12 bar blues backing track in E

⏪ PAST JAMS

JUNE 3, 2023
First electric jam!
Fear that amateurism + amplification would be a problem was unfounded. The public power outlets work. Strangers came and sat in. A guy brought his harmonica. The law didn't mind.
⚡ ELECTRIC
MAY 7, 2023
3rd jam.
5 acoustic guitars, 1 acoustic bass. Hard to hear each other at times — cemented the move toward electric. Notes taken. Lessons learned. Why have I still not taken photos or videos?
🎸 ACOUSTIC
APRIL 16, 2023
The one where strangers danced.
A stranger joined mid-song and started singing. Then a couple came and danced. Kids clapped along. People took photos. When it ended, everyone felt a little cool about what had just happened.
💙 MAGIC
MARCH 26, 2023
The debut.
Four acoustic guitar players who met through the Hilton Performing Arts Center advanced guitar class for military families. Northern Virginia's preeminent blues jam had officially begun.
